The economics of timing · Deterioration curve

Why timing is everything: $1 now vs $6–10 later

Pavement condition decays slowly, then collapses. Predicting the curve lets us treat in the cheap preventive window — before the steep drop into costly rehabilitation and reconstruction.

$1
Preventive · crack seal, fog seal, micro-surfacing in the 75–90 PCI window.
$4–5
Rehabilitation · mill & overlay once condition slips to 55–70 PCI.
$6–10
Reconstruction · full-depth rebuild after the curve collapses below 40 PCI.
RoadTwin advantage: predictive curves pinpoint the preventive window per-segment, keeping the network high & spend low.

Delivery · Phased deployment

From pilot to network-wide intelligence

A low-risk, staged rollout — value from the first corridor, scaling to a self-improving network twin without disrupting operations.

Phase 1

Baseline & pilot

Weeks 0–8
  • Mount sensors on pilot fleet (45-min install)
  • Scan priority corridor · build first twin
  • Calibrate against ROMDAS / GPR
  • Establish PCI / IRI baseline
Phase 2

Correlate & train

Months 2–4
  • Ingest historical condition & traffic data
  • Train & validate predictive models
  • Stand up GIS & dashboard integration
  • Onboard road-compound scientists
Phase 3

Decide & act

Months 4–8
  • Generate forecasts & treatment plans
  • Run multi-year budget optimisation
  • Push work orders to EAM / ERP
  • Extend to roadside-asset management
Phase 4

Scale & learn

Months 8+
  • Roll out across the full network
  • Continuous capture on every fleet trip
  • Track effectiveness · close the loop
  • Models self-improve each cycle
